MaidSafe Korea's first translation: Vault Explanation Video

This is our first translated video, translated from English to Korean. It is part of our active MaidSafe Korea Facebook page, and is now also released on the forums. This is the first of many translations / transcriptions to come. Please like and share!

International Students banned together to translate SAFE

Project SAFE will allow Secure Access For EVERYONE, but they won't be aware of it coming without translation into many other languages. We are friends at school and have been following SAFE for a long time, and want to help by getting the message out to everybody.

We have been working against our schedules to bring more translated documents to Project SAFE, and have began with a few videos and are now working with the papers. We will be on the forums and we would like for you to have discussions with us there to provide us more information and feedback throught the translation process. It is a joint effort to get the message out to the world, and we just want to do our part! It is a great community here and we are glad to join!

Also, we are looking to create a central hub for each language, so that people of different cultures can each have a place to go and possibly even discuss SAFE in their native tongue. We would appreciate any help in facilitating this! Thank you! Arigatou! Gamsahamnida!
